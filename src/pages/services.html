Our Services

Bellevue Pet Adventures is a locally owned and operated dog grooming, boarding, and daycare facility. We strive to provide the best in care, comfort and fun for your furry loved ones.

If you are interested in bringing your pup to us, please review the prerequisites by clicking the button below.

Boarding

We offer kennels in a variety of sizes in order to suit the needs and sizes of all breeds. Pets from the same household are welcome to share a single spacious kennel.

Grooming

Our top notch staff will have your dog looking and feeling their best. We offer hair trims, nail trims, and much more.

Daycare

Give your dog a chance to meet some new friends! Daycare provides dogs the ability to socialize and play with other dogs while our staff helps reinforce positive, social behavior.
