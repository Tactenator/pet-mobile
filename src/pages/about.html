Giving Dogs
The Time Of Their Lives

Bellevue Pet Adventures is a dog grooming, boarding, and daycare facility. Our mission is to provide the best experience for you and your pups by providing a safe, clean and fun environment for your dogs to enjoy!

Safety

Safety is our number one concern. Rest assured that we take every measure to ensure your dogs are healthy, secure and safe.

Cost

We provide your pets with the safe space they need while you're away without breaking the bank.

Medical Needs

Dog with medical needs? We got your back. Our trained staff will administer medication and monitor your dog's every need.

Home Sweet Home
Our trained staff makes your dogs feel like they were at home. We give them the love, care and attention they need to feel safe and have fun at our facility.
Stay Safe, Stay Healthy
All dogs will undergo a meet and greet as well as a health check to ensure that all dogs are allowed to play together safely.
Toys and Treats
We feel like your dog deserves to be pampered. We have a wide array of add-ons to be purchased to make your dog's stay that much more enjoyable.
