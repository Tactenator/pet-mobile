What We Offer

Bellevue Pet Adventures is a locally owned and operated dog grooming, boarding, and daycare facility. We strive to provide the best in care, comfort and fun for your furry loved ones.

If you are interested in bringing your pup to us, please review the prerequisites by clicking the button below.

Boarding

We offer kennels in a variety of sizes in order to suit the needs of all breeds. Pets from the same household are welcome to share a single spacious kennel.

Grooming

Our top notch staff will have your dog looking and feeling their best. We offer haircuts, baths, nail trims, and much more

Daycare

Give your dog a chance to meet some new friends! Day care provides dogs the ability to socialize and play while our staff helps to reinforce positive behaviors .
